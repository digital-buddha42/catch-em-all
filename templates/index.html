Pokemon Project

Explore our database of Pokemon and discover unique abilities and stats!

We've compiled data and information on all your favorite Pokemon!

This site was made to demonstrate our ability to create interactive data visualizations. We created all of our visualizations with data from a SQLite database, which was made by calling pokeapi.co. This project is powered by Flask and includes HTML/CSS and Javascript.

Thank you for visiting!
